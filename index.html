01 · Professional Profile

Who I am as an educator

Bio

Short professional biography
An enthusiastic educator dedicated to shaping well-rounded, globally-minded learners. With a B.A. in French Education and TRCN certification, I specialise in delivering the IPC curriculum while fostering linguistic and cultural appreciation through French. I believe in the power of reflective practice, data-informed instruction, and strong relationships to unlock each student's unique potential.

Teaching Philosophy

I believe every child has an innate ability to learn and thrive when provided with a safe, inclusive, and engaging learning environment. My teaching philosophy is rooted in creating learner-centred experiences that inspire curiosity, build confidence, and support every child's unique needs through active learning, differentiated instruction, and continuous assessment. I am passionate about fostering not only academic excellence but also creativity, resilience, and strong character. By embracing reflective practice, educational technology, and data-informed instruction, I strive to equip my students with the knowledge, skills, and values they need to become confident, lifelong learners in an ever-changing world.
